    Term element values from the LexInfo ontology (lexinfo:termElement).

    Classifies the morphological role of a sub-word element within a lexical entry (e.g. whether it is a prefix, suffix, or radical).

    Type Declaration

    • Readonlyaffix: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#affix"

      Generic affix — superclass of prefix, infix, and suffix.

    • ReadonlybaseElement: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#baseElement"

      Root or dictionary form of the word.

    • Readonlyinfix: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#infix"

      Morpheme inserted within a word stem.

    • ReadonlyinflectionElement: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#inflectionElement"

      Morpheme encoding inflectional information (declension/conjugation).

    • ReadonlymorphologicalElement: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#morphologicalElement"
