    Entry-level lexical relation property URIs from the LexInfo ontology.

    These are object properties whose domain and range are ontolex:LexicalEntry. Use the value as the JSON-LD property key when linking two entry nodes.

    Type Declaration

    • ReadonlyabbreviationFor: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#abbreviationFor"

      Links an abbreviated form to its expanded lexical entry.

    • ReadonlyacronymFor: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#acronymFor"

      Links an acronym to its expanded lexical entry.

    • ReadonlyclippedTermFor: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#clippedTermFor"

      Links a clipped/truncated term to its full form entry.
