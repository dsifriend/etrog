    Term-type values from the LexInfo ontology v3.0.

    TermType classifies the form or origin of a lexical entry rather than its grammatical role. It is a distinct dimension from part-of-speech and is set via the lexinfo:termType property.

    Type Declaration

    • ReadonlyabbreviatedForm: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#abbreviatedForm"

      Any abbreviated form (superclass of acronym, initialism, clippedTerm, contraction).

    • Readonlyabbreviation: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#abbreviation"

      A generic abbreviated form.

    • Readonlyacronym: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#acronym"

      An abbreviation pronounced as a word (e.g. 'NATO', 'laser').

    • ReadonlyCJK_compound: "http://www.lexinfo.net/ontology/3.0/lexinfo#CJK_compound"
